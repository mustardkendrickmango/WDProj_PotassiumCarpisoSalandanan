About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese shonen manga written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The story follows the life of Itadori Yuji, a high school student who happens to swallow a cursed object of the Jujutsu world and gain its powers. Ending up cursed by the swallowed object, Itadori is suddenly dragged into the thrilling world of Jujutsu sorcerers and cursed spirits.

The manga is composed of 30 volumes, its first chapter being published on March 5, 2018 and concluded on September 30, 2024. A prequel series entitled Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High was released in April 2017 and was only meant to be a short series, finishing after 4 chapters. Jujutsu Kaisen was released as a sequel series a year later, introducing new main characters.

An anime adaptation by Studio MAPPA was announced in November 2019 and officially began airing on October 3, 2020. Its second season was announced in February 2022 and aired from July to December, 2023. A third season is currently in the works, announced in August 2025 and will begin airing starting January 2026.

Also, another sequel series entitled Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo was announced on September 1, 2025, set 68 years after where the series ended and focuses on the grandchildren of two of the characters from the original series. The manga production is in progress and has 3 volumes.
